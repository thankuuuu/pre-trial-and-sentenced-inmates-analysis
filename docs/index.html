Overview

Welcome to pre-trial and sentenced inmates visualization and analysis website. This website is mainly focus on whether there are some contrast on pre-trial and sentenced inmates among different race and gender. This website will focus on small but significant scale by using the Department of Correction of the United State data of inmates who were pre-trial and sentenced held by Department of Correction facilities of US in 2018.

Purpose

The purpose of ths website is to use visualizations to observe the incarceration trends statistics of individuals being pre-trial and imprison after pretrial among different race and gender held by the Department of Correction facilities inmates in 2018. Hopefully, the visualization would help to identify some effect of racial and gender discrepancies toward pre-trial incarceration and imprison judgment.
